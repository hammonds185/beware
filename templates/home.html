About the service

With Beware, you can feel safe no matter where your adventures take you. You can look up the location of your future travels, or even just your current location and view the beware map to stay up to date with local incidents which have occurred in the area to facilitate your decision-making!

Beware Map

You can view incidents of various types. These types include crimes such as robbery/theft, housebreaking, and assault. We also include reports of discrimination, racial profiling, bad customer service, car accidents, and unwarranted police encounters. We use icons to keep your search simple.

Reports

As a member you can file your own reports about your experiences in an area. With your help we can keep our world happy and safe!
